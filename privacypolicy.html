Privacy Policy for Zen Void

Effective Date: 04-06-2025

Zen Void is a productivity and focus app developed by Revora Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide core app features. All data remains on your device unless otherwise stated.

a. App Usage Access

We access app usage information to detect which app is currently open. This allows us to block distracting apps during focus sessions. No data is sent or stored externally.

b. Overlay Permission

Used to show a blocking screen if a disallowed app is opened. No screen content or data is accessed or stored.

c. Contacts Access

Accessed only when you choose to allow calls from selected contacts or set SOS numbers. Your contacts data is never uploaded or shared and is used solely within the app.

d. Call Permission

Requested to allow direct calling to selected contacts. We do not monitor, store, or record calls or call logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

The permissions and data collected are solely used to support these features:

  • Blocking distracting apps during focus sessions
  • Allowing calls to selected contacts during sessions
  • Storing user preferences locally on your device
  • Providing reminders and motivational notifications

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Ads and Third-Party Services

We display ads using Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect usage data according to their policies.

  • To protect user privacy, especially for users under 18, non-personalized ads are served, which limits data collection.
  • For more details, please review Google AdMob’s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We take your data security seriously and follow best practices to protect it. All sensitive data is stored only on your device and is inaccessible to anyone else.

5. User Controls

You have full control over app permissions. You may revoke any permission at any time via your device settings. Disabling permissions may affect app functionality but will not affect your device’s normal operation.

6. Children’s Privacy

Zen Void is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

For users aged 13-17, we limit personalized ads and protect privacy according to applicable laws.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We encourage users to review this page periodically. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.
