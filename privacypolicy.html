Privacy Policy for Zen Void

Effective Date: 13-05-2025

Zen Void is a productivity and focus app developed by Revora Technologies. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information and protect your privacy when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the app's core features. All data remains on your device unless otherwise stated.

a. App Usage Access

  • We access app usage information to detect which app is currently open.
  • This is used to block distracting apps during focus sessions.
  • No data is sent or stored externally.

b. Overlay Permission

  • We use this to show a blocking screen if a disallowed app is opened.
  • No content or screen data is accessed or stored.

c. Contacts Access

  • We access contacts only when you choose to allow calls from selected contacts or set SOS numbers.
  • Your contacts data is never uploaded or shared and is only used within the app.

d. Call Permission

  • We request this permission to allow direct calling to selected contacts.
  • We do not monitor, store, or record calls or call logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

All collected permissions are used solely to support these features:

  • Block distracting apps using overlays
  • Allow calls to specific contacts during sessions
  • Store selected preferences on your device
  • Provide gentle reminders and motivational notifications

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We may display ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect usage data as per their policies.

See Google AdMob’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Data Security

We take security seriously and follow best practices to protect your data. All sensitive data is stored only on your device and is not accessible by anyone else.

5. User Controls

You are in full control of permissions. You may revoke any permission at any time from your device settings. Disabling permissions may affect app features, but not your device functionality.

6. Children’s Privacy

ZenVoid is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage users to review this page periodically. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
